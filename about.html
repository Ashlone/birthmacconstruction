Company Background

The Company is wholly (100%) South African Black owned. By exploiting the government’s favourable black empowerment policies, the Company seeks to firmly establish itself in the aforementioned areas of business, as well as, empower women and youths in the communities it does business.

Vision

-consistently provide the highest quality Pest Control service within budgetary parameters
-build lasting and quality relationships with our clients through personal attention and fair trade
-create practical and tailored approaches to our clients individual and specialized needs
-promote professionalism through constant training and improvement of staff skills
-partner with suppliers who share our work ethic and approach to business
-strive to make tomorrow better than yesterday for ourselves and our clients

Mission

Our mission is to provide an excellent and satisfying service, utilizing environmentally friendly methods and products. The environment and our clients, together with our workforce are our indispensable and most valuable assets Birthmac Constructions (Pty) Ltd is committed to excellence in satisfying our customers’ needs and strives for quality and safety. Our mission is to provide our customers with a one-stop service solution at the most convenient time and affordable rates

Values

-Being responsible corporate citizens, fulfilling our obligation as an integral member of society
-Provide products and services of uncompromising quality
-Seek mutually beneficial and enduring relationships based on unwavering commitment, openness, transparency and honesty
-Keep communication lines open and accurate, both internally and externally
-Respect – demonstrated through our attitude and care about feelings and rights of others
-Recognize / understand that personal dignity has nothing to do with race, culture, class, level of education or seniority, sexual orientation or seniority everybody with integrity has it
-Respect the law and social norms, aiming for harmony with society
-Our commitment to improving lives extends to the communities in which we live and work, where we focus on hunger relief, education and supporting local programs. In addition to offering scholarships and promoting community activities, we use our expertise in our service
